Josh Elizetxe

Former Fleischer Scholar and Fleischer Mentor

Josh is a successful serial entrepreneur and dedicated community leader. Josh is the founder of Foresold, a holding company focused on building high­ growth Internet brands. Each month, over 10 million people interact online with brands owned and operated by Foresold. Josh was the Valedictorian of his class at Alhambra and graduated Summa cum Laude from Barrett Honors College and W.P. Carey School of Business at ASU at 20 years old. In addition to being involved in improving his community, he is a sought after company advisor and serves on the board of the Phoenix Coding Academy.

Sebastian Navarro

Former Fleischer Scholar and Fleischer Mentor

Sebastian Navarro graduated in May 2015 from the W. P. Carey School of Business with a B.S. in Supply Chain Management. During his time at ASU, Sebastian served as both a business processes intern, as well as a global supply chain intern at Freeport-McMoRan Copper & Gold. Sebastian was also President of the Business School Council, a Success Team Leader, a Student Orientation Leader, McCord Scholar and Fleischer Scholars Mentor at ASU. He currently holds the position of Commodity Manager at American Airlines in Fort Worth, Texas. Sebastian is passionate about supply chain and would like to use his experiences to give back to communities around the world through humanitarian work in his chosen field.

Ashley Cano

Former Fleischer Scholar and Fleischer Mentor

Ashley Cano is a current student at the W. P. Carey School of Business pursuing a B.S. in Accountancy. While at ASU, she has achieved numerous accomplishments including becoming a member of the W. P. Carey Leaders Academy. She is also a Devils’ Advocate, responsible for giving tours to prospective students and their families. Ashley is a member of the National Honor Society and was recently selected as a McCord Scholar for the 2015-2016 school year. She was recently selected to star in the video “Day in the Life of a W. P. Carey Student” and is scheduled to graduate in May 2017.
